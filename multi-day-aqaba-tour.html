Multi-Day Aqaba Adventure Tour

Overview

Embark on an unforgettable multi-day adventure designed to showcase the diverse beauty and rich culture of Aqaba. From the vibrant underwater world of the Red Sea to the historical depths of the city and the majestic landscapes of Wadi Rum, this tour offers a comprehensive and immersive experience.

Quick Facts

Duration 3 Days / 2 Nights
Group Size Max 10 participants
Language English, Arabic
Difficulty Moderate (Includes walking and light hiking)
Inclusions Accommodation, all meals, transportation, entrance fees, and expert guide services.

Detailed Itinerary

  • **Day 1: Underwater Wonders of Aqaba**
    Morning: Snorkeling or introductory diving session in Aqaba Marine Park. Explore vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life. Afternoon: Glass-bottom boat tour for those who prefer to stay dry. Evening: Fresh seafood dinner at a local restaurant.
  • **Day 2: Historical & Cultural Immersion**
    Morning: Guided walking tour of Aqaba city, visiting Aqaba Fort, Sharif Hussein bin Ali Mosque, and bustling local markets. Afternoon: Traditional Jordanian cooking class or a visit to a local spice market. Evening: Authentic dining experience with a local family.
  • **Day 3: Wadi Rum Desert Safari**
    Full Day: Excursion to the majestic Wadi Rum desert. Enjoy a 4x4 jeep safari, explore ancient inscriptions, and marvel at the stunning rock formations. Option for a short camel ride. Evening: Return to Aqaba.

Your Guides to Authentic Aqaba

Welcome to our home! The Aqaba local experiences family is more than just a cooking class; we are your local connection to the heart and soul of Aqaba. We believe the best way to experience a culture is through its people, its food, and its hidden corners.

From the vibrant spices of our kitchen to the bustling energy of the local markets on our walking tours, and the shared stories over a traditional dining experience, we invite you to become part of our family. Come and discover the authentic local life with us.

Ramadan Iftar with a Local Family

Overview

Experience the heart of Jordanian culture by joining a local family in Aqaba for Iftar, the meal that breaks the fast during the holy month of Ramadan. This is more than just a dinner; it's an invitation into our home to share in a tradition of faith, family, and hospitality.

Quick Facts

Availability During Ramadan 2026 (Feb 18 - Mar 18)
Time Starts 30 minutes before sunset
Duration Approx. 2.5 Hours
Inclusions Traditional Iftar meal (dates, soup, main course, dessert), Arabic coffee, and tea.
Price 20 JD per person

What to Expect

  • The Breaking of the Fast: Begin with the traditional dates and water as the call to prayer sounds across the city.
  • A Festive Feast: Enjoy a multi-course meal featuring Ramadan specialties like Qatayef (sweet dumplings) and traditional main dishes.
  • Cultural Connection: Learn about the significance of Ramadan, local customs, and daily life in Aqaba through genuine conversation.
  • Authentic Hospitality: Experience the warmth and generosity that Jordanians are famous for, especially during this holy month.

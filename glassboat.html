Aqaba Glass Boat Tour

See the wonders of the Red Sea without getting wet! Perfect for families, couples, and all ages.

About the Experience

Embark on a relaxing glass-bottom boat tour in Aqaba and discover the vibrant underwater world of the Red Sea. Marvel at colorful coral reefs, tropical fish, and maybe even spot a sea turtle—all from the comfort of your seat. Our friendly local crew will share stories and point out marine highlights along the way.

Highlights

  • See Aqaba’s famous coral reefs and marine life through a crystal-clear glass bottom.
  • Perfect for families, kids, and anyone who prefers to stay dry.
  • Guided by experienced local boat captains.
  • Comfortable shaded seating and refreshments on board.
  • Photo opportunities of Aqaba’s coastline and the Red Sea.

What’s Included

  • 1-hour glass boat tour
  • Refreshments (water, juice, and snacks)
  • Life jackets and safety briefing
  • English and Arabic-speaking crew

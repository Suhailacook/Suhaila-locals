The Authentic Italian Pasta Masterclass

Overview

This immersive masterclass takes you deep into the heart of Italian culinary tradition. Learn the secrets to making perfect pasta dough from scratch, shaping three classic pasta types, and pairing them with authentic sauces. It's a hands-on, fun, and delicious experience for all skill levels.

Quick Facts

Duration 3.5 Hours
Group Size Max 10 participants
Language English
Inclusions All ingredients, a glass of wine, recipe booklet, and the meal you prepare.
Location The Culinary Studio, Downtown

What You'll Learn

  • **The Dough:** Mastering the classic "well" method for a perfect, silky-smooth pasta dough.
  • **Shaping Techniques:** Hands-on practice creating *Fettuccine*, *Ravioli*, and *Gnocchi*.
  • **Sauce Pairings:** Preparing a classic *Pesto Genovese* and a rich *Ragu Bolognese*.
  • **The Feast:** Sitting down together to enjoy the meal you created, paired with a glass of local wine.

Meet Your Host

Chef Maria Rossi

Chef Maria is a third-generation Roman chef with over 20 years of experience. She believes the best food is made with passion and simple, high-quality ingredients.

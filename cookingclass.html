Authentic Jordanian Cooking Class

Uncover the rich flavors of Jordan with a hands-on culinary journey in a warm, local setting.

Step into our family kitchen in the heart of Aqaba, where generations of traditional recipes come to life. This immersive experience is led by Agareed—our culinary matriarch—who guides you step-by-step.

What You'll Learn

Dive deep into the heart of Jordanian cuisine with our immersive cooking experience. Under the expert guidance of skilled local chefs, you’ll learn to prepare iconic dishes that are staple to Jordanian culture.

  • Mansaf: Jordan's national dish, a savory lamb and rice dish cooked in fermented dried yogurt sauce.
  • Maqluba: The famous "upside-down" dish with layers of meat, fried vegetables, and rice.
  • Fresh Tabbouleh: A vibrant and refreshing parsley and bulgur salad.
  • Authentic Appetizers: Learn to prepare traditional mezze like hummus or mutabal.

Experience Highlights

  • A comprehensive 3-hour guided cooking workshop.
  • All fresh, local ingredients and professional cooking equipment included.
  • Hosted in a welcoming, traditional home kitchen in Aqaba, offering an authentic cultural immersion.
  • Vegetarian-friendly and vegan options available upon request.
  • Small group sizes ensure personalized attention from our local experts.

What to Expect

  • A warm Jordanian welcome with traditional tea and sweets upon arrival.
  • Hands-on cooking with step-by-step guidance, suitable for all skill levels.
  • A delightful shared meal with your hosts and fellow guests, enjoying the fruits of your labor.
  • Take-home recipe cards to recreate your favorite dishes, along with local spice tips.
  • An unforgettable cultural exchange and a taste of true Jordanian hospitality.

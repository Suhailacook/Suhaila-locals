Our Journey

Discover the story, the passion, and the local family behind Aqaba's most authentic experiences.

Who We Are

Welcome to Aqaba local experiences! We are a family-run initiative in the heart of Aqaba, dedicated to sharing the warmth, flavors, and traditions of Jordanian hospitality. Our journey began with a simple idea: to open our home and hearts to travelers seeking genuine connections, delicious food, and unforgettable memories that you won't find in any guidebook.

Meet Suhaila

Founder & Culinary Matriarch

Suhaila, our founder, is deeply passionate about preserving traditional local recipes and sharing the warmth of Jordanian hospitality. With decades of home cooking experience, she welcomes visitors as lifelong friends, ensuring that every encounter is filled with laughter, learning, and delicious dishes.

Together with her family, Suhaila invites you to step inside their home kitchen, explore the local food markets, and experience the real culture of Jordan through the universal language of food.

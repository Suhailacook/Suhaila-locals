Historic Milestone: Jordan Qualifies for FIFA World Cup 2026!

Published on May 25, 2026 | By Suhaila Locals Team

A Dream Come True

On June 5, 2025, Jordan officially secured its place in the FIFA World Cup 2026 for the first time in history. The "Nashama" have finally broken through, marking a new era for Jordanian sports and national unity.

The streets of Amman, Aqaba, and every corner of the Kingdom erupted in joy as the final whistle blew during the qualifying rounds. For decades, Jordan has come close, but 2026 will be the year the world finally sees the spirit of the Nashama on the biggest stage of all.

The Journey to the World Cup

Jordan's path to the 2026 World Cup, to be held across Canada, Mexico, and the United States, was nothing short of legendary. Following a spectacular run in the 2023 Asian Cup where they reached the final, the team maintained their momentum under visionary leadership and a golden generation of players.

Key victories against regional powerhouses and a resilient defensive performance in the final rounds of the AFC qualifiers ensured that Jordan finished in the top two of their group, securing automatic qualification.

What This Means for Jordan

Football is more than just a game in Jordan; it is a passion that brings families and communities together. This qualification is a testament to the hard work, talent, and perseverance of the players and the unwavering support of the fans.

Local Insight: If you are visiting Jordan during the World Cup, expect an atmosphere like no other. Every café in Aqaba will be packed with fans, and the hospitality will be at an all-time high as we share our pride with the world.

Celebrating in Aqaba

Here in Aqaba, the celebrations have been non-stop. From the waterfront to the local markets, the red, white, black, and green of the Jordanian flag are everywhere. At Suhaila Locals, we are already planning special football-themed evenings and traditional meals to celebrate our team's success.

Preparing for the Big Debut

As the tournament approaches, the excitement continues to build. Jordan will be making its debut alongside other first-timers, and we are ready to show the world the "Nashama" spirit—courage, hospitality, and a never-give-up attitude.

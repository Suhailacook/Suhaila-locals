Best Diving Centre in Aqaba: Why We Recommend Coral Garden

Published on February 7, 2026

Are you ready to explore the hidden treasures of the Red Sea? If you're planning a trip to Jordan, diving in Aqaba is an absolute must. But with so many options, how do you choose the right partner for your underwater adventure? At Suhaila-Locals, we’ve done the research for you. Our top recommendation for the best diving experience in Aqaba is none other than Coral Garden Diving Center.

Local Tip: Coral Garden includes free underwater photos with their dives—perfect for sharing your adventure!

Why We Recommend Coral Garden Diving Center

Coral Garden isn't just another dive shop; it’s a PADI 5-Star Instructor Development Center (IDC) with a reputation for excellence. Here’s what makes them the best in the business:

  • World-Class Safety: Their team of expert instructors ensures you’re in safe hands while exploring the deep.
  • Luxury Boats: They operate two luxury boats, DESTINY and DESTINY PLUS, for the ultimate comfort at sea.
  • Comprehensive Courses: From beginner "Try Dives" to professional certifications, they cater to all levels.
  • Official Mares Distributor: Access to top-tier equipment and professional servicing.

How To Get From Aqaba Airport (AQJ) To Aqaba

Landing at King Hussein International Airport (AQJ)? Getting to the city center is straightforward if you know the local rates. We recommend taking a local taxi or pre-booking a shuttle for a smooth arrival.

The airport is located about 15-20 minutes from downtown Aqaba. Most travelers find the fixed-rate taxi system to be the most convenient way to start their journey.

  • Fixed Taxi Rate: 10 - 15 JOD
  • Travel Time: 15 - 20 minutes
  • 24/7 Availability at Arrivals
  • Private Shuttle Options Available
  • Direct access to Downtown & Hotels

Local Travel Tip

Always confirm the price with the driver before starting your journey. If you've booked a Walking Tour or Cooking Class with us, let us know your arrival time and we can help guide your driver to our location!

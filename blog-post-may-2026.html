Top 5 Things to Do in Aqaba in May 2026

Published on May 1, 2026

May is one of the most magical times to visit Aqaba. The weather is perfectly warm for both sea and land adventures, and the summer crowds have yet to arrive. Here are our top 5 recommendations for making the most of your trip to Aqaba in May 2026.

1. Dive into the Red Sea

The water temperature in May is ideal for diving and snorkeling. The coral reefs are teeming with life, and the visibility is excellent. Whether you're a seasoned diver or a beginner, you'll be mesmerized by the underwater world of the Red Sea.

2. Join a Local Cooking Class

Experience the heart of Jordanian culture by learning to cook traditional dishes. Our cooking classes are a great way to spend an afternoon, and you'll leave with a full stomach and a new set of culinary skills. Learn more about our cooking classes.

3. Explore the Aqaba Souk

The local market, or souk, is a vibrant hub of activity. In May, you'll find a wide variety of fresh produce, spices, and local handicrafts. It's the perfect place to immerse yourself in the local culture and find unique souvenirs.

The bustling Aqaba Souk.

4. Take a Sunset Boat Tour

There's nothing quite like watching the sunset over the Red Sea. A sunset boat tour is a relaxing way to end the day, and you'll get to see the stunning coastline from a different perspective.

5. Discover the History of Aqaba

Aqaba has a rich history that dates back thousands of years. Visit the Aqaba Archaeological Museum and the Aqaba Fort to learn about the city's fascinating past.

Local Tip: Don't forget to try a traditional Jordanian breakfast at a local restaurant. It's a delicious way to start your day!
